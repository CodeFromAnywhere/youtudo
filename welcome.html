YouTudo

Get YouTube video transcripts with a simple URL hack!

How it works:

  1. Find a YouTube video you want the transcript for
  2. Replace "youtube" with "youtudo" in the URL
  3. Press Enter to get the transcript

Example:

Original YouTube URL:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

Modified YouTudo URL:

https://www.youtudo.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
